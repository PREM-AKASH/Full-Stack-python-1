2. Print Even and Odd numbers with a given integer 'n'

soln:
                                                  # Program with a for loop and an 'if' statement
n=int(input("Enter the number:"))
for i in range(0,n):
	if i%2==0:
		print("Even Number")
	else:
		print("Odd Number")
